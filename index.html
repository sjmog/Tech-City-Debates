Tech City Debates

Monthly debates on issues in tech, happening in and around 'Tech City' in London. Entrepreneurs, Academics, Investors, and Special Interests come together to debate motions that are relevant to the immediate and long-term future of the tech community, and the wider world. Debates occur in the Mace format.
